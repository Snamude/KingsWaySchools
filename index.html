Welcome to Kings Way Schools

We Invest In Whole-Person Education and Christian Values

Founded in 2001 by **Mr. Frank Muramura** and **Mrs. Loy Muramura**, Kings Way Schools has spent over two decades fostering academic superiority and practical independence. Based along Entebbe Road, Kampala, we maintain a legacy where solid discipline is considered the cornerstone of lasting academic success.

Our administrative structure guarantees close personal monitoring, child-centered mentoring, and outstanding academic preparations. We are unconditionally committed to shaping God-fearing, academically elite, and responsible leaders equipped to change society positively.
